About Spotifriend

Are you bored at home listening to music by yourself? Well, now you can meet new people all over the world with the same music taste! All you have to do is login to your Spotify account and let our algorithms do the rest.

Spotifriend will rank your top songs, artists, and genres and match you with others with similar preferences, allowing you to meet new people and vibe over music together.
